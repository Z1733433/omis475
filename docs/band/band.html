My favorite albums:
Random Access Memories
RAM as it has been dubbed by the Daft Punk community, is an album full of great songs that put a modern spin of the groups classic electronic style.
Alive 2007
Alive 2007 is a special album that contains only live music from the groups "Alive 2007" concert. All songs on the album are remixes of many of the duo's most popular songs put together with masterfull style.
Human After All
Human after all was the album that brought Daft Punk to the surface, specifically with their hit song Human After All. Although dated, many of the songs are still considered to be some of Daft Punk's finest works.
